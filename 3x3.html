Methods

There are 3 principal speedcubing methods: CFOP, ROUX, ZZ.

CFOP

CFOP is the most common method used in speedcubing and consists of 4 steps:

  1. Cross
  2. F2L (First 2 Layer)
  3. OLL (Orientation of the Last Layer) [57 alg]
  4. PLL (Permutation of Last Layer) [21 alg]

ROUX

ROUX consists of 3 steps:

  1. FB (First Block)
  2. SB (Second Block)
  3. CLL (Corner of the Last Layer), there's 2 options:
    • CMLL (Corner without M of the Last Layer) [42 alg]
    • COLL (Corner Orientation of the Last Layer) [42 alg]
  4. L6E (Last 6 Edges)

ZZ

ZZ consists of 3 steps:

  1. EO (Edge Orientation) + Cross
  2. F2L (First 2 Layer) without cube rotation
  3. LL (Last Layer), there's 2 options:
    • ZBLL (ZB Last Layer) [493 alg]
    • COLL (Corner Orientation of the Last Layer) [42 alg] + EPLL (Edge Permutation of Last Layer) [4 alg]
